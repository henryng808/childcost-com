By June 2026, AI is no longer a "future" technology—it is the primary operating system of the global economy. In **The AI-Ready Parent**, James Chen provides a comprehensive guide for parents who want to ensure their children are not just users of AI, but masters of it.

From Literacy to Mastery

Chen argues that the traditional educational path is being fundamentally disrupted. He introduces the concept of **Co-Intelligence**—the ability to work seamlessly alongside AI systems to solve complex problems.

Core Competencies for 2026:

  • Prompt Engineering for Kids: Teaching the logic of instruction and intent.
  • Critical Verification: Learning to spot hallucinations and biases in AI-generated content.
  • Creative Synthesis: Using AI as a canvas for high-level creative projects.
