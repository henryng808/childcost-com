In her critical July 1, 2026 release, Digital Sovereignty for Families, Dr. Sarah Miller provides the definitive framework for protecting the family unit from the "algorithmic erosion" of modern tech. The Neurological Hedge

Miller identifies the "dopamine liability" that comes with unchecked device usage and provides a tactical manual for building "cognitive sovereignty" in children. Her focus is not on total disconnection, but on intentional engagement. She provides parents with the data needed to understand how addictive loops impact developing brains and offers practical systems to rewire the family for focus and connection.
