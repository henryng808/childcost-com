The Manifesto: Jonathan Haidt's "Anxious Generation"

While released slightly earlier, Jonathan Haidt's **The Anxious Generation** has reached its peak influence in 2026 as the foundational text for the "Great Rewiring." Haidt provides the scientific and sociological evidence needed to make the hard choice: reclaiming a play-based childhood.

  • Collective Action: How families can band together to create screen-free communities, reducing the FOMO that drives tech addiction.
