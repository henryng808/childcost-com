The Six Dimensions of Responsibility:

  • Emotional Stewardship: Moving from managing a child's feelings to teaching them emotional self-governance.
  • Economic Literacy: Integrating financial reality into daily parenting—crucial in our high-inflation era.
  • Digital Sovereignty: Establishing the rules of engagement for a screens-first world.
  • Social Contract: Teaching the value of contribution over consumption within the family unit.
