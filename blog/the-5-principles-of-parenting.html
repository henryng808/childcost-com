The Pain Point: The Noise of "Perfect" Parenting

The Solution: The Core 5 Framework

Dr. Aliza Pressman, a developmental psychologist, cuts through the noise. She argues that you don't need a thousand rules. You need 5 principles that work in almost every situation.

The 5 Core Principles:

  • 🧠 Relationship: The foundation of everything. If the connection is broken, nothing else works.
  • 🧠 Reflection: Understanding your own triggers and history before reacting to your child.
  • 🧠 Regulation: Managing your own emotions so you can help your child manage theirs.
  • 🧠 Rules: Setting clear, compassionate boundaries that provide safety.
  • 🧠 Repair: The most important one. How to fix things when you inevitably mess up.
