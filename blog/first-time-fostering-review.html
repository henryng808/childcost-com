The Support Deficit in Foster Care

Foster parenting is perhaps the most rewarding yet challenging form of caregiving. While the emotional stakes are high, the administrative and systemic support for foster parents often feels fragmented. In 2026, as child care costs rise and public systems face budget constraints, foster parents need more than just heart—they need a tactical roadmap to navigate trauma, bureaucracy, and emotional self-regulation.
