The War for Attention

Pfeiffer explains how modern media structures are designed to exploit our most basic fears and biases.   • The Truth Protocol: A family framework for verifying information before believing it.
  • Value-Based Communication: How to talk about difficult topics in a way that prioritizes empathy over anger.
