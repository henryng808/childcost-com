Lisa See has long been the chronicler of the hidden lives of women and the profound, often difficult, bonds between generations. The Currency of Connection

While the setting is historical, the themes are strikingly modern. See explores the "unseen labor" of parenting—the emotional and physical toll required to build a future for the next generation. For today's parents, the novel serves as a mirror, reflecting the same resilience and fierce protection required to navigate the economic uncertainties of 2026.
