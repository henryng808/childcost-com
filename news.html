Child Care Prices Now Rival Major Household Expenses

New data from Child Care Aware® of America reveals a startling trend: child care costs have officially crossed the threshold to become one of the top three monthly expenses for American families, often exceeding rent or mortgage payments.

The report indicates that supply is failing to keep pace with demand, driving prices to "unmanageable" levels in over 40 states. For many middle-income families, the annual cost of child care for two children now exceeds the average annual mortgage payment by nearly 25%.

"We are reaching a breaking point where the financial stress is directly impacting parental mental health and family stability," warns advocacy director Linda James.
